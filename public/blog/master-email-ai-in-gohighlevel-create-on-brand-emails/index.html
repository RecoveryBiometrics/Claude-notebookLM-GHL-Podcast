How to Set Up and Configure Email AI

Setting up Email AI takes less than five minutes. Here's the exact process:

Step 1: Access the Email Builder
Log into your GoHighLevel account and navigate to the Email module. Click "Create New Email" and you'll see the option to use AI or start from scratch.

Step 2: Define Your Email Parameters
Before AI generates content, you'll provide critical context:

  • Email subject (optional—AI can generate this too)
  • Email purpose (promotional, nurture, sales, transactional, etc.)
  • Target audience (who are you talking to?)
  • Desired action (what should the reader do?)
  • Tone preference (professional, casual, urgent, etc.)

Step 3: Input Your Prompt
Write a brief description of what you need. Example: "Create a follow-up email for a dental clinic targeting leads who viewed our teeth whitening service. Include a special offer for first-time patients. Keep tone professional but friendly."

Step 4: Generate and Review
Click the generate button. AI produces your email in seconds. Review for accuracy, edit as needed, and save.

💡 Pro Tip

The more specific your prompt, the better your email. Don't just say "write a sales email." Say: "Write a sales email for a SaaS platform targeting mid-market HR managers. Highlight ROI, include social proof, and end with a free demo CTA." Specificity = better output.

Creating On-Brand Emails with AI

Generic AI emails don't convert. Your brand voice matters. Here's how to ensure every AI-generated email sounds like you.

Set Your Brand Guidelines in GoHighLevel
In your account settings, document your brand voice. Examples:

  • "We speak to busy entrepreneurs. Keep sentences short. Use "you" frequently."
  • "Our tone is educational but not preachy. We use data and case studies."
  • "Avoid corporate jargon. Use conversational language like we're texting a friend."

Use Templates for Consistency
Create email templates with your brand colors, fonts, and footer information. When AI generates content, it fills into your existing template, maintaining visual consistency instantly.

Generate Variations and Choose the Best
Don't settle on the first draft. Ask AI to generate 2-3 variations with different angles:

  • Variation 1: Pain-focused (highlight the problem)
  • Variation 2: Benefit-focused (highlight the solution)
  • Variation 3: Urgency-focused (create scarcity)

Review all three and pick what resonates most with your brand.

Integrating Email AI into Your Workflows

The real power of Email AI emerges when you integrate it into automation workflows. Here's how:

Using Email AI in Automation Sequences
When building a workflow, you'll add an Email Action. Instead of manually writing each email, use the AI feature to generate it on the fly. This is perfect for:

  • Lead nurture sequences: AI generates follow-ups based on lead behavior
  • Sales automation: Different emails trigger based on where prospects are in your funnel

Creating Dynamic Content with AI
GoHighLevel's Email AI respects merge tags. You can personalize AI-generated content with {{FirstName}}, {{Company}}, or custom fields. AI adapts the message around your personalization, keeping it natural and relevant.

Best Practices and Common Mistakes

I've seen agencies save thousands per month with Email AI—and I've seen others waste time because they skipped critical steps. Here's what actually works:

✓ Do This:

  • Always review AI output before sending. Don't automate blind.
  • A/B test AI-generated subject lines against your own.
  • Update your brand guidelines in GoHighLevel regularly.
  • Use AI for first drafts, then layer in your personal touches.
  • Monitor email metrics. If open rates drop, adjust your prompts.

✗ Avoid This:

  • Don't use vague prompts. "Write a sales email" will produce generic garbage.
  • Don't send AI emails without editing. Your readers can tell.
  • Don't assume one version works for all audiences. Segment and customize.
  • Don't forget compliance. Ensure GDPR and CAN-SPAM rules are followed.

Optimizing Workflows After AI Creation

Mastering Email AI is just step one. The real wins come from optimizing the entire workflow around it.

Track Performance Metrics
After your first week of AI-generated emails, review:

  • Open rates (target: 25%+ for cold, 40%+ for warm)
  • Click rates (target: 3-5%)
  • Unsubscribe rates (anything above 0.5% needs investigation)
  • Conversion rates (tie to revenue when possible)

Refine Your Follow-Up Logic
Don't just fire and forget. Design workflows with:

  • Click tracking: If someone clicks, move them to a sales sequence
  • Open tracking: If they don't open, trigger a second attempt
  • Delay optimization: Test 1-hour, 24-hour, and 72-hour send delays

Test and Iterate
Run A/B tests on AI-generated variations. Does AI-written copy outperform your manual writing? Measure it. Use winning email angles to refine future AI prompts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Email AI to create entire campaigns?

Yes. You can generate subject lines, body copy, CTAs, and even email sequences. However, always review and personalize. AI provides the skeleton; you add the soul.

How does Email AI handle client branding if I run an agency?

Create separate brand guidelines for each client within GoHighLevel. When you switch clients, update the context in your prompt, and AI adapts its tone and messaging accordingly. This keeps emails on-brand while saving hours of manual writing.

Will Email AI replace my email copywriter?

Email AI replaces the time your copywriter spends on first drafts—not the strategic thinking. Your copywriter becomes a strategist and editor, not a typist. This means faster campaigns and better ROI.

What if I don't like the AI-generated email?

Regenerate it. Ask for a different angle or tone. The more you use Email AI, the better you get at prompting. It's a skill, not magic.
