How to Create Ads Directly from WhatsApp Conversations

WhatsApp is often where your best campaign ideas come from—clients brainstorm, share real-time feedback, and mention what's working. GoHighLevel lets you capture that momentum instantly.

Step 1: Identify the Ad-Worthy Message
Look for messages where a client is describing a product, service, or promotion. It could be a new offer, a testimonial repackaged as a value prop, or a hot topic they want to advertise.

Step 2: Click the Create Ad Button
Next to or within the WhatsApp message thread in GoHighLevel, you'll see a breadcrumb button labeled "Create Ad" or similar. Click it. GoHighLevel immediately captures the message content, any media attached, and the sender's context.

Step 3: Choose Your Ad Platform
You'll be prompted to select whether you want to run this as a Facebook ad, Google ad, or another supported platform. Select based on where your target audience spends time and where your budget performs best.

Step 4: Configure Campaign Details
GoHighLevel's lightweight editor lets you refine the copy, add visuals if none were attached, set your daily budget, and define your target audience (geographic location, age, interests, behaviors). You can also set campaign duration and optimization goals (clicks, conversions, engagement, etc.).

Step 5: Review and Launch
Preview your ad exactly as it will appear on the platform, then hit launch. Your ad goes live within minutes, and all tracking and reporting funnels back into your GoHighLevel dashboard.

How to Turn Social Posts into Paid Ads Instantly

Your Social Planner is where you schedule and post content for clients. GoHighLevel's integration means high-performing posts can be promoted as paid ads without recreating them.

When to Promote a Post: After a post goes live, monitor its initial performance. If engagement (likes, comments, shares) spikes faster than expected, that's a signal to boost it with paid promotion. Alternatively, if you've crafted a post that aligns directly with a sales objective (a webinar sign-up, product launch, limited-time offer), you might promote it immediately.

Using the Breadcrumb Button: In your Social Planner feed, locate the post you want to promote. Next to it, you'll see a "Promote" or "Create Paid Ad" button. Click it. GoHighLevel pulls the post's copy, images, and video (if applicable) and bundles it into an ad format ready for Facebook or Instagram.

Set Your Budget and Audience: The lightweight setup form asks you to define your target audience, daily budget, and campaign objective. Because the ad copy and creative are already optimized (they performed organically), you're leveraging proven messaging. This typically means faster ROI and lower cost-per-result.

Monitor and Optimize: Once live, the ad reporting syncs to your GoHighLevel dashboard. You'll see impressions, clicks, engagement, and conversions (if your funnel is tracking them) in real time. Pause underperformers and scale winners within the platform.

Setting Up Your Ad Manager for Facebook and Google Ads

Before the breadcrumb buttons work, your Ad Manager needs to be configured and authenticated. Here's the setup checklist:

Connect Your Facebook Business Manager Account: In GoHighLevel, navigate to Settings → Integrations → Facebook. You'll be prompted to log into your Facebook Business Manager account and grant GoHighLevel permission to manage ads on your behalf. Select which ad accounts you want to connect (you can add multiple for different clients).

Connect Your Google Ads Account: Go to Settings → Integrations → Google Ads. Sign in with the Google account associated with your Google Ads manager account, then grant permission. Choose which Google Ads accounts GoHighLevel can access.

Verify Your WhatsApp Integration: Ensure your WhatsApp Business Account is linked to GoHighLevel. This is done in Settings → Integrations → WhatsApp. Your phone number and account must be verified with Meta.

Confirm Your Social Planner Connection: If you're using GoHighLevel's Social Planner, verify that it's connected to your social accounts (Facebook Pages, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.). This happens automatically when you first authorize GoHighLevel to post on your behalf, but double-check in Settings → Social Accounts.

Once all integrations are live, the breadcrumb buttons will appear throughout WhatsApp and Social Planner. No additional setup is needed.

Context Preservation: When you use the breadcrumb button, GoHighLevel preserves the original message, tone, and intent. You're not writing ads from scratch; you're amplifying conversations and content that already resonated.

Reduced Errors: Manual ad creation invites copy-paste mistakes, formatting issues, and misaligned audiences. One-click creation standardizes the process and reduces human error.

Better Retargeting: Since your ads are created and tracked inside GoHighLevel (not in external ad managers), all conversions, leads, and customer data flow back into your CRM. This makes retargeting seamless—your warm audiences are automatically identified and can be re-engaged with follow-up campaigns.

Pro Tips for Maximizing Your Ad Campaigns

Test Multiple Variations: Create two or three ad versions from the same WhatsApp conversation or post. Change the call-to-action, headline, or audience slightly. Let them run for 3-5 days, then pause the underperformers and double down on winners.

Link Ads to Your Sales Funnel: When you create an ad in GoHighLevel, make sure the landing page, form, or link points to a GoHighLevel funnel or campaign page. This ensures every click is tracked and every lead is automatically added to your pipeline for nurturing.

Use Engagement Ads for Social Proof: Posts with comments and shares already act as social proof. When you turn these into paid ads, they inherit that proof. Users seeing comments and reactions are more likely to engage and convert.

Monitor Cost Per Result Daily: GoHighLevel's Ad Manager dashboard updates in real time. Check it daily in the first week of any campaign. If your cost per lead or conversion is trending up, pause and investigate—it might be a targeting issue or ad fatigue.

Stack Ads with Email and SMS Sequences: When an ad converts and a lead enters your CRM, immediately trigger an email or SMS sequence. GoHighLevel's automation lets you create workflows that nurture ad-driven leads without manual follow-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create ads from any WhatsApp message?

Yes and no. The breadcrumb button appears on messages in your GoHighLevel inbox. However, not every message is ad-worthy—it works best for messages where someone is describing a product, offer, or piece of content. Messages that are purely logistical or conversational won't convert well into ads.

What if I want to edit the ad copy before it goes live?

Absolutely. After you click the breadcrumb button, GoHighLevel opens an editor where you can refine the copy, adjust the headline, change visuals, and rewrite the call-to-action. The original WhatsApp message is just your starting point.

Do I need to have a Facebook or Google Ads account already set up?

Yes. You'll need an active Facebook Business Manager account (for Facebook and Instagram ads) and/or a Google Ads account (for Google Search, Display, and YouTube ads). GoHighLevel connects to these existing accounts; it doesn't create new ones for you.

How long does it take for an ad to go live after I launch it?

Usually 5-30 minutes. Both Facebook and Google review ads for policy compliance. Simple, straightforward ads often launch in under 15 minutes. Complex ads or those with sensitive keywords might take up to an hour.

Can I track conversions from ads created in GoHighLevel?

Yes. As long as your landing page or funnel has GoHighLevel's tracking pixel or form, every conversion will be logged in your CRM. You'll see ROI, cost per lead, cost per sale, and other metrics directly in your GoHighLevel dashboard.
