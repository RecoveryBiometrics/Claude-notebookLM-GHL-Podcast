What is the Voice AI Chat Widget?

The Voice AI Chat Widget in GoHighLevel is a browser-based feature that allows website visitors to have real-time voice conversations with your AI agent without picking up a phone. It's built directly into your website or funnel page, eliminating friction from the lead capture process.

Unlike traditional phone calls that require commitment, voice chat feels more conversational and casual. Visitors can ask questions, get instant answers, and qualify themselves in real-time. For your business, this means automatic lead qualification happening 24/7, even when your team is offline.

The widget captures detailed conversation data—including what was discussed, visitor tone, and engagement level—so your sales team knows exactly where each lead stands before they follow up.

Enable Voice AI Chat Widget from Labs

Voice AI Chat is still relatively new in GoHighLevel, which means it lives in the Labs section where experimental features live. Here's how to unlock it:

Step 1: Navigate to Settings
From your GoHighLevel dashboard, click Settings (usually in the left sidebar). Look for an account-level settings option, not sub-account settings.

Step 2: Find the Labs Section
In Settings, scroll down until you find Labs or Beta Features. This is where GoHighLevel keeps unreleased and experimental tools.

Step 3: Enable Voice AI Chat Widget
Look for the Voice AI Chat Widget toggle and switch it ON. You should see a confirmation that the feature is now enabled for your account.

💡 Pro Tip

If you don't see Voice AI Chat Widget in Labs, contact GoHighLevel support. It may not be available on your plan yet, or your account may need to be flagged for access.

Configure Your AI Agent for Voice Conversations

Before you can add the widget to a page, you need to set up or configure an AI Agent to handle the voice conversations. Here's the process:

Step 1: Access AI Agents
Go to Settings → AI Agents in your GoHighLevel account. You'll see a list of any existing agents or an option to create a new one.

Step 2: Create or Select Your Voice Agent
If you already have an AI Agent set up for text chatbot, you can use that same agent for voice. If not, click Create Agent and give it a name like "Lead Qualification Agent" or "Customer Support Agent."

Step 3: Define the Agent's Personality and Instructions
In the agent settings, you'll write the system prompt—this is what tells the AI how to behave. Example:

"You are a friendly lead qualification specialist for [Your Business]. Ask visitors about their biggest challenge, their timeline, and their budget. Be conversational, warm, and helpful. Always offer to connect them with a team member if they're interested."

Step 4: Set Up Knowledge Base (Optional but Recommended)
Upload FAQs, service descriptions, or product information so your AI agent has accurate details to reference. This prevents the agent from making up information.

Step 5: Configure Lead Capture Fields
Decide what information the agent should collect: name, email, phone, company, industry, budget, timeline, etc. The agent can ask for this information naturally during conversation.

Add the Widget to Your Website or Funnel

Once your AI Agent is configured, it's time to deploy the Voice AI Chat Widget. Here's where the magic happens:

Step 1: Find Widget Settings
Navigate to Funnels & Pages → [Your Page/Funnel] → Settings or look for a Widgets or Chat section in page settings.

Step 2: Select Voice AI Chat Widget
Choose the Voice AI Chat Widget option and select the AI Agent you want to use for this widget.

Step 3: Customize Widget Appearance
Most implementations let you customize:
• Widget button color and icon
• Opening message (greeting text)
• Position on page (bottom-right is standard)
• Size and animation style

Step 4: Enable for Website (Optional)
If you want the widget on your external website (not just GoHighLevel funnels), go to Settings → Widget Code and copy the embed code. Paste this into your website's header or footer HTML. The widget will then appear on all pages where you install it.

Step 5: Save and Publish
Once configured, save your changes and publish the funnel or page. The widget is now live.

Test and Deploy Your Voice AI Chat

Before sending traffic to your widget, test it thoroughly. Real voice quality and AI responses directly impact your lead capture rate.

Testing Checklist:

  • ✓ Click the widget button and confirm the voice chat initiates
  • ✓ Speak clearly and confirm the AI understands your words
  • ✓ Listen to the AI's response—is audio quality clear?
  • ✓ Ask a few test questions and verify the agent stays on-brand
  • ✓ Confirm the widget properly captures your information at the end of the conversation
  • ✓ Check that lead data appears in your CRM with conversation notes
  • ✓ Test on mobile devices (this is critical—most visitors will use phones)

If audio quality is poor or the AI is giving bad answers, go back to your agent settings and refine the instructions or knowledge base before going live.

Best Practices for Lead Capture with Voice AI

1. Keep Opening Greetings Short
Your AI should introduce itself in 10-15 seconds max. Long intros cause visitors to close the widget. Example: "Hi! I'm Alex, your AI assistant. What brings you here today?"

2. Ask Qualifying Questions Naturally
Don't feel like a survey. Weave qualification into the conversation. Instead of "What's your budget?", try "What are you currently spending on [solution]?"

3. Enable Fallback to Human Agents
If the AI detects the visitor wants to talk to a real person, seamlessly hand off to your sales team. This prevents frustration and increases conversions.

4. Use Conversation Data for Sales Follow-Up
Your CRM should capture not just contact info, but a transcript of what was discussed. Sales reps can use this to personalize their follow-up and move deals faster.

5. A/B Test Your Agent's Tone
Create two versions of your agent—one more formal, one more casual—and test which resonates with your audience. Run this test for at least 100 conversations before deciding.

6. Monitor Abandonment Rates
If visitors are closing the widget early, it usually means the opening question isn't relevant or the AI responses feel robotic. Adjust and test again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to integrate Twilio to use Voice AI Chat Widget?

No. The Voice AI Chat Widget uses voice-over-internet (VoIP) directly in the browser. You don't need Twilio or any third-party phone service. However, if you want to use a Voice AI Phone Agent (calls initiated by the AI), then Twilio integration is required. For widget-based voice, it's built-in.

Can I add the Voice AI Chat Widget to multiple pages?

Yes. You can add the same widget to different funnels and pages, or create multiple widgets with different AI agents for different purposes (e.g., sales qualification vs. customer support). Each widget is independently configured.

What happens if the visitor's browser doesn't support voice?

GoHighLevel typically has a fallback option, usually switching the widget to text-based chat. Make sure this fallback is enabled in your widget settings so no one is left without a way to engage.

How are visitor conversations stored and how is data privacy handled?

All voice conversations are transcribed and stored in your GoHighLevel account. Conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. GoHighLevel is compliant with GDPR and CCPA, but make sure you disclose voice recording in your privacy policy and get consent from visitors before they use the widget.

Can I train my AI agent with custom information about my business?

Yes. Use the Knowledge Base feature to upload documents, FAQs, service details, or pricing info. Your AI agent will reference this information during conversations, reducing hallucinations and keeping responses accurate to your business.
